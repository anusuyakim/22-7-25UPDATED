Case Study

AI-Powered Logistics Platform

Revolutionizing supply chain management for a leading e-commerce provider with predictive route optimization and real-time inventory tracking.

The Challenge

Our client, a rapidly growing e-commerce retailer, was facing significant operational hurdles. Their manual route planning led to inefficient delivery schedules, increased fuel costs, and frequent delays. Furthermore, a lack of real-time inventory visibility resulted in stockouts and overstocking, impacting customer satisfaction and tying up capital. They needed a unified, intelligent platform to streamline their entire logistics operation.

Our Solution

Vendhan Info Tech engineered a cloud-native logistics management platform from the ground up. The core of the solution was a machine learning model that analyzed historical traffic data, weather patterns, and delivery loads to predict the most optimal routes in real-time.

  • Developed a React-based dashboard for real-time fleet tracking and visualization.
  • Built a Python/Flask back-end with a custom-trained TensorFlow model for route optimization.
  • Integrated IoT sensors on delivery vehicles for live data feeds.
  • Deployed the entire system on a scalable AWS infrastructure using Docker and Kubernetes.

Key Outcomes

22%

Reduction in fuel consumption and delivery times.

15%

Decrease in inventory holding costs.

30%

Increase in on-time delivery rate.
