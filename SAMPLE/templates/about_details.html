Our Journey

Founded in 2020, Vendhan Info Tech was born from a simple yet powerful idea: to build a technology company where innovation, excellence, and inclusivity are not just ideals, but the very fabric of our operations. As a proud women-led enterprise, we set out to challenge the status quo, proving that a diverse team is a strong team.

From our humble beginnings in Dindigul, we have grown into a dynamic force, delivering cutting-edge solutions to clients across the globe. Our journey is one of relentless learning, strategic growth, and an unwavering commitment to our clients' success.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional, human-centric technology solutions that solve complex business challenges, drive sustainable growth for our clients, and create a positive impact on society. We are committed to fostering a culture of innovation and empowering the next generation of women in technology.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized leader in technology and a symbol of empowerment, known for our engineering excellence, ethical practices, and our role in building a more inclusive and equitable digital future for all.
